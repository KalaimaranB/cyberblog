Courses

APSC100 - Applied Science 100

An introduction to engineering, including topics like: roles, responsibilities, diciplines, sustainability, prototyping, CAD & ethics

APSC160 - Applied Science 160

Introductory programming course in C with practical application shown using Arduinos. Focused on understanding core programming principles & snytax.

MATH100 - Math 100

Differential Calculus with Applications is one of 2 mandatory math courses focused on teaching varying techniques for differential computations.

MATH101 - Math 101

Integral Calculus with Applications is the 2nd of two mandatory math courses focused on teaching varying manual techniques for integral computations.

MATH152 - Math 152

Linear Systems is an introductory math course teaching 2D/3D geometry, vectors, matrices, eigenvalues & related concepts.

PHYS157 - Physics 157

Introductory Physics for Engineers I teaches core concepts including heat, thermodynamics, oscillations, waves & sound.

PHYS158 - Physics 158

Introductory Physics for Engineers II focuses on Electricity & Magnetism along with DC/AC circuits.

PHYS170- Physics 170

Mechanics I focuses on static & dynamic analysis of bodies and related concepts including impulse, work, energy among others.

CHEM121 - Chemistry 121

Structure & Bonding in Chemistry discusses fundamental bonding theories & structural chemistry, with modern applications. Some concepts were highlighted in the accompanying lab component.

CHEM123 - Chemistry 123

Thermodynamics, Kinetics & Organic Chemistry teaches those concepts with a specific application relevant to modern society. Some concepts were highlighted in the accompanying lab component.

BMEG101 - Biomedical Engineering 101

Introduction to Biomedical Engineering provides a foundational introduction to the 4 stream of BMEG: Cellular Engineering, Systems & Signals,Biomaterials & Biomechanics & Informatics

BMEG102 - Biomedical Engineering 102

Biomedical Engineering lab teaches basic laboratory skills in genetic engineering of bacteria, bioinformatics pipeline construction & electrical instrumentation.

BMEG201- Biomedical Engineering 201

Technical Communication for Biomedical Engineers teaches students about report preparation, oral presentation among others underscoring the interdisciplinary nature of BMEG.

BMEG210- Biomedical Engineering 210

Thermodynamics in Biomedical Engineering builds of first year chemistry and physics, focusing on the laws of thermodynamics with applications to biomedical systems.

BMEG220- Biomedical Engineering 220

Circuits & Electromagnetics with Applications to Biomedical Engineering builds off of first year physics. This course teaches electrostatics, electromagnetics, capacitance/inductance int eh context of physiological & biomedical systems.

BMEG230- Biomedical Engineering 230

Biomechancis I involves studying two dimensional movement of humans, focusing on applying mathematical techniques to compute forces & moments about bones, ligaments & other biomechanical structural components.

BMEG245- Biomedical Engineering 245

The Fundamental Units of Life: From Cels To Systems provides an introudction to cell structure & function. The accompanying lab provided hands-on experience with Image Analysis, DNA Manipulation using Plasmids & Informatics tools.

BMEG250- Biomedical Engineering 250

Cellular Physiology & Biophysics builds off of BMEG 245 and the introduction of cellular processes. It focuses on the mathematical & digital modelling of differential equations that reflect biological systems such as drug diffusion.

BMEG257- Biomedical Engineering 257

Biomedical Engineering Design I focuses on engineering design in the context of BMEG, and introduces relevant ethics & sustainability principles.

MATH253- Math 253

Multivariable Calculus teaches students about partial & directional derivatives, Lagrange multiplies, multiple integrals and other related concepts.

MATH256- Math 256

Differential Equations focuses on linear ordinary differential equations while introducing Laplace Transforms, Fourier series & the separation of variable technique for partial differential equations.

MATH264- Math 264

Vector Calculus for Electrical Engineering was taken alongside BMEG 220. This course teaches concepts such as divergence, curl, Gauss & Stokes theorem as well as applications to electrostatics & magentostatics.

MATH302- Math 302

Introduction to Probability is a course that teaches core notions of probability, random variables, limit theorems & more.

CPEN221- Computer Engineering 221

Software Construction I is a core course that teaches design, implementation & reasoning about software systems. It delves into specification design, testing methodologies, object-oriented design through 3 major projects.

MICB212- Microbiology 212

Currently Enrolled: Introductory Immunology & Virology teaches about the innate & adaptive immune responses, allergies, virus structure & replication cycle and some data analysis.

CHEM233- Chemistry 233

Currently Enrolled: Introduction to Organic Chemistry builds off of CHEM123 and focuses on the structure, bonding & physical properties of aliphatic & aromatic compounds. It discusses chemical reactivity of common functional groups as well.
